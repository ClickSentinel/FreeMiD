# Code of Conduct

FreeMiD follows a simple community standard:

- Be respectful
- Be constructive
- Assume good intent until shown otherwise
- Do not harass, threaten, or discriminate against other contributors or users

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Hate speech or discriminatory conduct
- Deliberate sabotage of issues, pull requests, or discussions
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or close contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in a ban from participation.

If you need to report a serious issue, use the security contact process in `SECURITY.md`.
